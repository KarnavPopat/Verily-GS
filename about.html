Who Are We?

Verity Today is an online based newspaper and newsletter that especially targets the new, upcoming generation. It was founded by Sohani Wahi, a 10th grade student, and currently the team stands strong with a strength of 100+ students from different age groups. Verity Today plans to create a forum where opinions and views can be expressed without any harsh lashbacks.

Given the coming times, the circulation of print media has sadly gone down immensely, but it remains more vital than ever to stay updated. Verity wishes to provide this basic information to our readers in a way that interests them.We hope to make this a very interactive forum for everyone by asking for inputs and suggestions from our readers so that you too, can have something which in a way is yours too.

One main motive/slogan is "a little something for everyone" . We want our newsletter to have something of interest to everyone, thus bringing us to our wide range of departments.

Verity started simply as a thought which turned into a reality. It was inspired by different student-led organisations that have a huge place in our thoughts and mesmerised us into wanting to start something of our own.

Not only does Verity provide information and regular news updates on important subjects like global affairs and business, we also have departments like entertainment and creativity for you to enjoy as well as to support growing artists.
